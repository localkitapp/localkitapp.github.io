A persistent scratchpad in your browser

Create, edit and pin notes that persist across browser sessions using localStorage. No account required, no cloud sync — your notes live on your device.

LocalKit · Quick Notes
Quick Notes · 100% local · nothing uploaded

Features

  • Auto-save — notes save automatically 600ms after you stop typing
  • Pin notes — pinned notes always appear at the top of the list
  • Search — filter notes by title or body content instantly
  • Word count — live word count shown in the editor footer
  • Persistence — notes survive tab closes and browser restarts

Keyboard shortcuts

ShortcutAction
Ctrl/Cmd + NNew note
Ctrl/Cmd + SSave immediately
Ctrl/Cmd + FOpen search
EscClose search
Notes are stored in localStorage — they are specific to the device and browser profile. Clearing browser data will delete your notes.
