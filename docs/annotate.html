How the blur tool works

The blur/redact tool pixelates a selected region by sampling the original screenshot at a lower resolution and drawing it back at full size. This creates a mosaic effect that obscures text and faces without completely hiding that content existed.

For truly sensitive data, use a solid rectangle in black instead of the blur tool. Pixelation can sometimes be partially reversed.
